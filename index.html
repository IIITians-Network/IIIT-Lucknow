    IIIT Lucknow


    FOUNDATION

    The Indian Institute of Information Technology, Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh is one of the new IIITs proposed by the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India.IIIT Lucknow had initiated its functioning in Allahbad in August 2015, under the mentorship of IIIT Allahbad.IIIT Lucknow then shifted to it's permamnent campus in IT City,Lucknow in 2019.

    STUDENT LIFE

    Since the shifting of the campus 7 Cultural and 6 technical clubs and along with them an E-Cell has been started.Studendts have been taking active part in cultural and technical activites and winning laurels.In the previous academic session the students have hosted a Technical Fest-Equinox, an Entreprenuership summit-Start the Buck Up and a national MUN Conference. In the coming year we will also be witnessing our cultutral festival-Solstice and a sports festival.
